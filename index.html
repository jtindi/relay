Why This Matters

take back control

Synaptic Relay, LLC was developed to help patients regain control of their medical images.

There are 5,534 registered hospitals in America. Out of that number 3,231 of those hospitals are in secured networks that are very protective of data and unwilling, in almost all cases, to help the patient free themselves from the networks. This is the way they control a person's medical care decisions.

who we are

Synaptic Relay, LLC is a company of physicians that wants to free you from these controlled networks. We will leverage our medical expertise to legally and conveniently provide patients their medical images in a secure digital format. We will encourage patients to securely store their images in a manner that allows rapid access from any location globally.
