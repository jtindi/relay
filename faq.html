How do we do this

A blockchain is a digitized, decentralized, public ledger of all cryptocurrency transactions. Constantly growing as ‘completed ’blocks(the most recent transactions) are recorded and added to it in chronological order, it allows market participants to keep track of digital currency transactions without central record keeping. Each node (a computer connected to the network) gets a copy of the blockchain, which is downloaded automatically.

Which crypto wallet?

An advanced wallet to manage cryptocurrencies and tokens. Many of these features are firsts to enable consumers to send and receive currencies and tokens, build an address list, send by name rather than by address, and provide end-to-end encrypted messaging from an intuitive interface.

Why Pillar Wallets?

With the help of Synaptic Relay, LLC, it is our hope that people will begin to take responsibility for their medical images and securely store them for their lifetime. A significant number of patients who might benefit from having their images available are unable to get access to or find the images when needed. By obtaining and maintaining a digital copy of your images you can keep them forever and have them readily available whenever you need them. The days of having to call a hospital network and request your films and physically pick up a CD or USB drive are over. Get your medical images digitally from Synaptic Relay, LLC.
